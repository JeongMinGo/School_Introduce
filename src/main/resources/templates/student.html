Welcome! Student Center Building

Administration Building was completed in 1974

Woojeongdang is a 3th floor building next to the museum. Originally,
the second floor and land were directly connected to the stairway,
but it became what it is after breaking the stairs to build the academy.
It used to be used as a student center during the days of Seoul Women's Normal University, the predecessor of Sejong University, but it is now used for many purposes.
On the first floor, there is a large food court, where several menus are sold every day, and next to them are GS25 convenience stores where you can buy many things simply.
In front of it, there is a shop called Brookly where you can buy toast, hot dogs, and coffee.
In the old days, there was a place called Saenghyeop instead of Gs25, but it disappeared at the end of 2014.
On the second and third floors, there is a professor's office, a club room and a small theater.
