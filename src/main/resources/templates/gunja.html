Welcome! Gunja Building

Gunja Building was completed in july 1959 and provides students with a space for learning

This building is the oldest building in this campus.
College of Arts and Physical Education is usually use the building.
In particular there are two restaurants in the building at first basement floor and the sixth floor.
The restaurant on the first basement floor is famous for a hangover dish, Sundubu Jjigae.
Many student go this restaurant after drink a lot to hangover.
The restaurant on the sixth floor is reserved for faculty members. But it open for college students.
It is the only one buffet restaurant in campus, so it can little bit expensive.
On the first floor, there are several facilities.
Woori-Bank and ATMs, a bookstore, an optician, a post office and copy machines that students can use are on the first floor.
There are many students at the beginning of the semester to buy books for lecture and to issue the student id card.
At first, there was no elevator, but it was installed after built the building.
So elevator location is not comfortable.
