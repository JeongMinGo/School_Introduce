Welcome! Union Building

Sejong University Student Center was completed in February 2010 and has been a rest area for students so far.

2st floor Configuration : Jeju Mole Bang Cafe, Rock Climbing
On the second floor, there is JeJu Mole Bang, Lounge for Foreigners and an artificial rock climbing room
Juju Mole Bang is the cafe that ingredients for drinks sold in cafes are from Jeju Island.
Both price and taste are really resonable.
An artificial rock climbing rooms entrance is prohibited.
If you want to enter the room there is a permission from Sejong University.
1st floor Configuration : CU Convenience Store, Job Cafe, Restaurant
B1st floor Configuration : Restaurant
On the first basement floor, there is a famous student restaurant at Sejong University.
The restaurant also appeared on several TV shows for the delicious dish.
The most famous dish is salt-pork-fired rice and shrimp-fried rice.
The restaurant is open from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m. on weekdays and only until 2 p.m. on Saturdays.
There is a shower room on the second floor of the building, but towels and supplies are not supported.
There are many club rooms from the third floor to sixth floor.
If you want to entrance the club, it is good choice to look around these floors.
