Yongdeokgwan is a 4th floor building with one basement level.
The construction was completed in October 1996 and will be up to its current size after a series of additions in February 2003.
It is a lecture hall in the Department of Dance and Physical Education at the College of Arts and Sports.
Classrooms and laboratories are located on the first floor, and on the second floor are ballet rooms and modern dance rooms for dance and students.
On the first basement floor, there are judo fields, kendo taekwondo halls, training halls, aerobics halls and squall markets for students of physical education.
The expansion will be made as the campus development plan was approved by the foundation's board of directors held on Feb. 23, 2017.
