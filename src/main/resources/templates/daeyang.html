Welcome! Daeyang AI Center Building

Daeyang AI Center Building was completed in march 2019 and provides students with a space for learning

Deayang AI Center is the latest building to be built.
Convenience facilities are scheduled from the ground floor to the second floor, and only Twosome Place is currently standing by.
From the fourth to eighth floors above the ground, Department of Computer Science professors' labs are located.
So you can see many professor in this building.
Guest houses and multi-purpose halls are scheduled for the other 9th to 12th floors, but they are still under construction.
There are many laboratories and lecture rooms in the first basement floor and the second basement floor
Most of the lectures at the building are for software colleges.
On the second basement floor, there is a shower room that is cleaner than the shower room of the student center, But no one knows about it.
On the second basement floor, there are offices of Sejong University Lifelong Education Center.
So there are some a dancing practice room and design labs.
