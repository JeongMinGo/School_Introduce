Welcome! GwangGaeto Building

GwangGaeto Building was completed in April 2005 and provides students with a space for learning

This building is the tallest building in Sejong University.
College of Administration, Hotel Tourism College and Natural Science College are usually used this building.
On the first basement floor, there is a cafe and GS25 convenience store, and an exhibition hall. Exhibition hall is available if you apply with the student support office before a month
On the 1st to 13th floors, there are many classroom for liberal lecture.
On the fifth floor, there is a cafe that has been remodeled from classroom No. 514.
So the cafe name is 514.
At first, it started with welfare of hotel management students. But it became famous so it opend for everyone. And It is now run by a graduate of Sejong University.
The price is a little expensive, but it tastes great. And recently, Park Jun Young of GOD in Youtube Wassup Man came in this cafe.
On the second basement floor, there is large convention room, so many events are held.
In particular, there are many academies and pairs. So some events are good for students.
